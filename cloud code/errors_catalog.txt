
current_sensor_error:
    0: all of the current sensors are OK.
    1 : current sensor#1 or current sensor#2 is defect.

voltage_sensor_error;
    2 : all of the voltage sensors are OK.
    3  : voltage sensor#1 is defect.
    4  : voltage sensor#2 is defect.
    5  : voltage sensor#3 is defect.
    6  : voltage  sensor#1 is defect.
    7 : the voltage sensor 1 &2 & 3 maybe defect.

heat_sys_error:
    8 : Heating system is OK.
    9  : Heating system is defect. 
    
cool_sys_error:
    10 : cooling system is OK.
    11  : cooling system is defect.


cell_error:
    12 : all cells are connected and there is no defect.
    13  : cell #1 is not connected, short circuited, over-charged or over-discharged.
    14  : cell #2 is not connected, short circuited, over-charged or over-discharged.
    15  : cell #3 is not connected, short circuited, over-charged or over-discharged.
    16 : cells #1 and #2 are not connected, short circuited, over-charged or over-discharged.
    17 : cells #1 and #3 are not connected, short circuited, over-charged or over-discharged.
    18 : cells #2 and #3 are not connected, short circuited, over-charged or over-discharged.
    19 : cells 1#, #2, and #3 are not connected, short circuited, over-charged or over-discharged.
